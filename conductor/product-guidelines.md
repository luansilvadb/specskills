# Product Guidelines

## Design Principles

1. **User First**: Every decision should prioritize user experience
2. **Simplicity**: Keep interfaces clean and intuitive
3. **Performance**: Optimize for speed and efficiency
4. **Accessibility**: Design for all users

## Quality Standards

- All product features must address clear user problems and match the defined target audience.
- Accessibility standards must be met on every feature launch.
- All major user-facing changes must be documented in the Decision Log below.

## Skills Manifesto
As we build the Conductor, we embrace the **Skills Framework**—a philosophy that empowers the AI to temporarily adopt profound, specialized mindsets rather than acting purely as a general assistant. All significant new capabilities and behavior adjustments should be distilled into standalone "Skills" (stored in `conductor/skills/`), allowing us to scale the agent's intellect organically and maintain absolute focus and premium quality in execution.
