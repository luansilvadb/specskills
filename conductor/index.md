## Templates Structure

All project templates are organized in the `conductor/templates/` directory:

- **Track Templates**: `spec.md`, `plan.md`, `track-index.md` - For defining and managing development tracks
- **Project Configuration**: `product.md`, `tech-stack.md`, `workflow.md` - Core project setup files  
- **Guidelines**: `product-guidelines.md` - Product guidelines and principles
- **Index**: `index.md` - Project overview and tracking

These templates can be customized to fit your project's specific needs. After running `/setup`, templates are copied from the system defaults to `conductor/templates/` where you can modify them.
